# Hackathon Rules

Welcome to the **Build with Blockchain: CSK Hackathon**.

Please read the following rules carefully before participating.

---

## 1. Eligibility

This hackathon is open to students and members of the CSK community.

Participants may compete individually or in teams.

---

## 2. Team Size

Teams may consist of **1–4 members**.

Participants without teams can find teammates in the hackathon communication channels.

---

## 3. Project Requirements

Projects must:

• Align with the hackathon theme  
• Demonstrate how blockchain technology is used in the solution  
• Be original work created during the hackathon period

Pre-existing ideas are allowed, but the project development must occur during the hackathon.

---

## 4. Participation Tracks

Participants may choose one of the following tracks:

### Idea Track
Concept-based solutions and presentations.

### Prototype Track
Product designs or system mockups.

### Builder Track
Working blockchain-based applications.

---

## 5. Judging

Projects will be evaluated by invited judges based on:

• Problem relevance  
• Innovation  
• Blockchain integration  
• Presentation quality

Each track will be judged independently to ensure fairness.

---

## 6. Code of Conduct

Participants must maintain a respectful and collaborative environment.

Plagiarism, harassment, or disruptive behavior will lead to disqualification.

---

## 7. Ownership

Participants retain ownership of their projects.

However, by participating, teams agree that their projects may be showcased by CSK for educational or promotional purposes.

---

Good luck and happy building!
