# Build with Blockchain: CSK Hackathon March 2026 🚀

Welcome to the official repository for the **Build with Blockchain: CSK Hackathon**, organized by the Computer Society of Kirinyaga (CSK).

This two-week hackathon invites students to explore the possibilities of blockchain technology by designing or building innovative solutions that address real-world problems.

---

## 🌍 Theme

**Web3 for Student Innovation & Social Impact**

Participants are challenged to create blockchain-powered solutions that improve student life or contribute to solving problems within communities.

---

## 📅 Timeline

Hackathon Start: **16 March**

Week 1:  
• Team formation  
• Idea development  
• Project planning  

Week 2:  
• Building or prototyping  
• Preparing presentations  

Final Day:  
• Project presentations and judging

---

## 🧩 Participation Tracks

To ensure inclusivity for participants of different skill levels, the hackathon will have three tracks:

### Idea Track
For conceptual blockchain solutions.

Participants will present:
- problem statement
- proposed blockchain solution
- system design or workflow

No coding required.

---

### Prototype Track
For teams creating a product prototype.

Participants may submit:
- UI/UX mockups
- system architecture
- user journey designs

Tools like design software are allowed.

---

### Builder Track
For teams developing technical implementations.

Participants may build:
- smart contracts
- decentralized applications
- blockchain-based tools

---

## 🏆 Judging Criteria

Projects will be evaluated based on:

• Problem relevance  
• Innovation and creativity  
• Use of blockchain technology  
• Presentation quality

More details can be found in **RULES.md**.
